    MAGIC OF

    THE WEEKND

The Weeknd has been named the world's most popular artist by Guinness World Records.
The "Blinding Lights" singer has 111.4 million monthly listeners on Spotify, which is more than any other artist..
Insider has listed some of the 33-year-old superstar's other achievements.
The Weeknd has sold over 17 million albums worldwide.
3 of his 5 studio albums have topped the charts in the United States.
He's also had 8 No. 1 singles.
In total, his songs have been streamed 40.7 billion times on Spotify.
